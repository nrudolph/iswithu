The Artist

Sound as a feeling

isWithU crafts immersive sonic landscapes at the intersection of electronic music, ambient texture, and raw emotional resonance. Born from late-night sessions and a deep love for sound design, each track is a journey through mood and memory.

Drawing from a palette of synthesizers, field recordings, and vocal fragments, the project explores what it means to truly be present — with yourself, with others, with sound.
